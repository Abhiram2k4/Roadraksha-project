Driven by Safety, Powered by Community

Road Raksha was born from a simple idea: that technology can bridge the gap between citizens and authorities to create safer infrastructure for everyone.

Our Mission

We aim to reduce road accidents and vehicle damage caused by poor road conditions. By providing a transparent, real-time platform for reporting and tracking road hazards, we ensure that every voice is heard and every pothole is accounted for.
